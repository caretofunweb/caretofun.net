**Sora** is an advanced **text-to-video model** developed by OpenAI. Its primary goal is to understand and simulate the physical world in motion, bridging the gap between textual prompts and dynamic visual content. Here are the key features of Sora:

1.  **Text-to-Video Generation**: Sora can generate videos up to a minute long while maintaining visual quality and adhering closely to the user’s input.
